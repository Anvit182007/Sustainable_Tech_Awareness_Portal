Welcome to the Sustainable Tech Awareness Portal

The world is increasingly reliant on the internet, yet few of us think about the environmental cost of our digital habits. Every online search, video stream, and social media post contributes to a growing carbon footprint. At the Sustainable Tech Awareness Portal, we aim to educate users about the hidden impact of their online activities and offer practical solutions for reducing their digital footprint. Explore our resources to learn about greener alternatives and how you can make a difference in today's connected world.
